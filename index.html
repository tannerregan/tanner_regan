Economics of Greenfield Urban Planning

In Dar es Salaam, there is over supply of large lots in new greenfield developments. Reallocating land towards smaller lots would improve formal land access for more and poorer households and raise aggregate welfare.

No Shame in My Name

In Kampala, reporting tax delinquents raises payment but disseminating information demotivates others, while recognizing compliers backfires -- apparently driven by a preference to hide wealth, rather than delinquency status.
