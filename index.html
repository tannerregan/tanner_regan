Housing, Property Rights, and Urban Development in Growing Cities

I am an economist interested in Urban and Development Economics. My research covers housing, property rights, urban planning, and property tax in developing country cities. I received my Ph.D. from the London School of Economics, and my M.A. and B.Sc. from the University of Toronto.
