Tanner Regan

Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics and the Elliott School of International Affairs at George Washington University.

I am an economist interested in Urban and Development Economics. My research covers housing, property rights, urban planning, and property tax in developing country cities.

I received my Ph.D. from the London School of Economics, and my M.A. and B.Sc. from the University of Toronto.
